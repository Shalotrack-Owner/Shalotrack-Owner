# 🚀 ShaloTrack
### Smart GPS Tracking & Fleet Management Platform

ShaloTrack is a Sri Lankan GPS tracking and vehicle security platform providing real-time vehicle tracking, fleet management, and intelligent monitoring solutions for businesses and individual vehicle owners.

> Our goal is to make vehicle tracking simpler, faster, and more reliable through modern technology and innovative software solutions.

---

## 🌍 About ShaloTrack

ShaloTrack delivers GPS tracking solutions for:

| Vehicle / Asset | |
|---|---|
| 🚗 Cars | 🚐 Vans |
| 🏍 Motorcycles | 🚚 Trucks |
| 🏢 Commercial Fleets | 📦 Assets & Equipment |

The platform combines GPS technology, cloud infrastructure, mobile applications, and fleet management tools to provide secure and reliable vehicle monitoring.
